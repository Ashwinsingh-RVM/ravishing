NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and agreements herein contained, the parties agree as follows:

1. DEFINITIONS

  1. "RVM" means the Reverse Vending Machine installed by the Service Provider for collection of eligible beverage containers.
  2. "Premises" means the location specified in Schedule A where the RVM is installed.
  3. "DRS" means the Deposit Return Scheme as notified by the Government of Goa.

2. TERM

  1. This Agreement shall commence from the date of installation of the RVM and shall remain in force for a period of three (3) years.
  2. The Agreement may be renewed by mutual consent of both parties.
  3. Either party may terminate this Agreement by giving sixty (60) days written notice.

3. OBLIGATIONS OF THE SERVICE PROVIDER

  1. Install, operate, and maintain the RVM at its own cost.
  2. Provide regular collection of deposited containers.
  3. Ensure the RVM is operational during agreed hours.
  4. Maintain cleanliness around the RVM installation area.
  5. Provide necessary training to designated personnel of the Host.
  6. Carry out repairs and maintenance as required.
  7. Maintain adequate insurance for the equipment.
  8. Comply with all applicable laws and regulations.

4. OBLIGATIONS OF THE HOST

  1. Provide the agreed space as specified in Schedule A.
  2. Ensure electricity connection is available (single phase, _____ watts).
  3. Ensure internet connectivity (WiFi/Ethernet) is available.
  4. Provide covered shelter/shed for the RVM if not already available.
  5. Ensure security of the RVM during non-operational hours.
  6. Allow access to Service Provider's personnel for maintenance.
  7. Not relocate or tamper with the RVM without prior approval.
  8. Report any damage or malfunction to the Service Provider promptly.
